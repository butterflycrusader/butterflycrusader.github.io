What These Documents Show

Hendersonville deployed Flock Safety AI surveillance cameras with no public input. Key findings:

  • 16 cameras photographing every vehicle
  • $48,000 per year ongoing cost
  • Data stored 30-90 days
  • Camera locations kept secret from public
  • Minimal oversight (internal audits only)
  • Data can be shared with other agencies
